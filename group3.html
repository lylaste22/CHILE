CHILE 2015201

Chile-Harvard Innovative Learning Exchange 2015

Task Description

The goal of our team was to develop a classifier of different types for non moving stellar objects. Our input data were time series of 20 epochs (4 per night during 5 nights) for star-like sources, we not include extended sources like galaxies, and auxiliary information provided by cross-match with 2MASS survey. We want to distinguish between variable and non variable objects and then classify subclasses of variable like binaries stars, pulsating stars and non periodic objects like quasars and supernovae.

Group 3: Zhijie (Sabrina) Zhou, Yingzhuo (Diana) Zhang, Jorge Martinez, Catalina Espinoza
